Welcome to the Prexcel website

This website contains the project reports and all other relevant information about Prexcel

"A desktop app that aims to help you make better presentations and improve your online presentation skills."


1. Presentation Analysis & Detailed Feedback : Either through live recording or external uploading of a video, the video is analysed and detailed feedback is given based on a visual and speech analysis.


2. Live Feedback : Prexcel gives live feedback when the user is recording their presentations using the recording functionality.


3. Tutorial : The app contains a tutorial page and functionality, which includes information about making good presentations. There is a chatbot instead of searching for particular information on the tutorial page


4. User Accounts and Progress Tracking : Users can create accounts and use the app with their accounts. They can see their past presentation grades and they can track their improvement.
