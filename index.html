ABOUT PREXCEL

Key features of our application

Analysis & Feedback

Either through live recording or external uploading of a video, the video is analysed and detailed feedback is given based on a visual and speech analysis.

Live Feedback

Prexcel gives live feedback when the user is recording their presentations using the recording functionality.

Tutorial

The app contains a tutorial page and functionality, which includes information about making good presentations. There is a chatbot instead of searching for particular information on the tutorial page.

Accounts & Progress

Users can create accounts and use the app with their accounts. They can see their past presentation grades and they can track their improvement.
